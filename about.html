Our Purpose

Why we built EduLearn

EduLearn was founded in 2021 by a group of educators and technologists who saw a gap: online learning platforms were either too expensive, too shallow, or too disconnected from real-world needs.

We set out to build a platform where expert knowledge meets genuine community — where you don't just watch videos, but actually build skills you can use.
